Welcome to Halcyon Massage and Wellness

We are skilled massage therapists here to help find relief and remedies for your aches and pains

How to Find Us:

520 Main Street (US Rte 20) in the second block of buildings east of Waltham City Hall, between Appleton and Cross St. We are on the 2nd floor above the dentist offices.

West bound, we are just past Wilson's Diner on the left. East bound, we are just past New Covenant Church on the right.

There is parking in front of the building and ample parking behind the building. The driveway to the rear parking lot is on the right side of the building. It is little hard to see as it is tight to the side of the building and bordered by tall bushes and a tree.

You can enter the building through the main lobby or either of the rear entrances. If you park in the rear of the building the exterior stairway to the second floor rear entrance is the most direct route to our office

Note: If you find the front entrance locked on evenings or weekends, use the the second floor rear entrance.

We hope to see you soon.
